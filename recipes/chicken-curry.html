Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a simple dish packed with flavour.

It is relatively light and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of Chicken breast
  • 150 grams of white rice
  • 1 jar of 'some curry sauce'
  • 1 bell pepper of your choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Turn two hobs to medium heat
  2. Pour 1 tablespoon of olive oil into the pan, and pour 350mL of water into the pot
  3. Place the pan on one hob and the pot on the other
  4. Pour the rice into the pot once the water has started to boil
  5. Chop the Chicken breast into small pieces and place them into the pan
  6. Using a different knife, or after cleaning the current one, chop the bell pepper
  7. Crack over some salt and pepper and leave the chicken to brown on one side
  8. Once the Chicken has browned, turn over and add more salt and pepper
  9. After the chicken has browned, pour the curry sauce over the chicken
  10. Once the curry sauce comes to a boil, stir and turn down the heat to medium low
  11. While the chicken is simmering, add the bell pepper chunks to the curry sauce
  12. After 20 minutes of total cooking time, remove the pan and the pot from the hob
  13. Strain the rice and place into a bowl, then top with the chicken curry
