Stir Fry

Chicken stir fry is a simple dish packed with flavour.

It is relatively light, quick to make and is cheap!.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of Chicken breast
  • Stir Fry Sauce
  • 1 packet of noodles
  • 1/2 of a bell pepper of your choice
  • 50g of carrots
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place a large pan onto a hob on medium high heat
  2. Coat the pan in a small layer of oil
  3. Chop the Chicken breast into small pieces and place them into the pan
  4. Using a different knife, or after cleaning the current one, chop the bell pepper and carrots into small strips
  5. Crack over some salt and pepper and leave the chicken to brown on one side
  6. Once the Chicken has browned, turn over and add more salt and pepper
  7. Once the chicken has browned completely, remove from the pan and add in the carrots
  8. Add the bell pepper strips and some broccoli
  9. Add the noodles and cook for a further two minutes
  10. Add the chicken back into the mix and add the sauce
  11. Once the sauce begins the boil, turn the heat down to medium low and simmer for two minutes
